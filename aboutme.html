Avatar

Avatar is one of the most creative pieces of cinematography and has made more things in film achievable. CGI has made it possible to create entire worlds and scenes that could never have been achieved with traditional effects. The movie has many sweeping shots that excited viewers with the soaring view of Pandora Every Na'vi costume was built in real life. James Horner invented many of the instruments used in the movie's score. Cars factored into Cameron's creature design. Cameron used two different 3D camera rigs to shoot Avatar.
